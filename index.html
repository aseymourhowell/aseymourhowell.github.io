About me

I am a Postdoctoral research fellow at Chonnam National University in Gwangju, South Korea, as part of the LFANT group. Previously, I was a research associate at the University of Bristol, working with Min Lee. I also completed my PhD at the University of Bristol under the supervision of Andrew Booker.

My current research is in computational number theory, mainly in the theory of automorphic forms and trace formulae.
